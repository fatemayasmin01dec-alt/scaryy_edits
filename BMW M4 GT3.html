BMW M4 GT3

The BMW M4 GT3 is a premier customer racing car designed by BMW M Motorsport to dominate GT3-class endurance and sprint racing.
Introduced in 2022 to replace the M6 GT3, it features a P58 3.0-liter straight-six turbocharged engine producing up to 590 hp.
It is renowned for its excellent drivability, lower operating costs, and a, according to, "steering wheel co-developed with Fanatec" that allows for direct configuration.
The platform has seen massive success globally, with an "Evo" version launched for 2025 that features improved aerodynamics, lighter weight, and refined handling.
